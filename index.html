How to Scout

Check that the match number and team are correct (can be changed if an error is found)
Hit Continue

Once match begins, record when your robot picks up and places a game piece
When your robot picks something up, press the correct button in their loading zone
The robot on the center of your field should show said item
press the location on the grid that corresponds with where your robot placed

If you tap a field location by accident, hit Ignore Click
After the Auto period, you will need to hit the Teleop button located between the Low and High shot trackers
If your robot climbs, tap the appropriate bar on the field diagram to indicate its ending height.
If your robot plays defense, mark how they did at the end of the match.
Filling in the Extra Notes section is optional, but helpful
